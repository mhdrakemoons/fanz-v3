## Overview

Reduce the scope so you can be consistent. Consistency creates signal; signal compounds reach.

## How it works

Pick a single platform, one clear outcome (click, reply, save), and ship daily.

## Cadence

Week 1: baseline. Week 2: tighten hooks. Week 3: iterate offers.
