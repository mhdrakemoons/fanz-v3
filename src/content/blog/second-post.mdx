## Before you sell

Audience clarity matters more than offer complexity. Pick one person, one outcome, one channel.

### Offer ladder

1. Lead magnet (free)
2. Starter product ($29-$49)
3. Flagship ($199-$499)
4. Advisory ($1,000+)

> Monetization accelerates when your free content is outcome-driven.

## Pricing table

| Tier | Price | Promise |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | $49 | Quick win in 14 days |
| Flagship | $299 | Transform in 8 weeks |
| Advisory | $1,500 | Done-with-you clarity |

### What to track

- Email list growth
- Lead magnet conversion rate
- CAC and LTV

Set one weekly metric and ship.
