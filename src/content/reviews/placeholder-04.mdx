## TL;DR

Elegant calendar that shines with Notion databases; weaker for deep automation.

## Setup

Instant if you already live in Notion. Database linking is the magic.

## Best for

Small teams mapping content from idea → draft → published without complex workflow engines.

## Verdict

Keep Google Calendar for life; use Notion Calendar to visualize content pipelines.
