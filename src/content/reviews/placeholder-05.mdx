## TL;DR

Solo creators move fastest in Canva; teams building reusable systems win in Figma.

## Templates vs systems

Canva’s breadth of templates accelerates ship speed. Figma enables design tokens, components, and variants that scale.

## Verdict

Pick based on collaboration needs: templates (Canva) vs. design system power (Figma).
